## Goals

These are strict product goals.

Primary Goals

Convert academic material into structured hierarchical knowledge.

Generate expandable mind maps from uploaded content.

Provide adaptive explanation styles:

Analytical

Real-world

Humorous

Movie analogy

Maintain strong JSON structure consistency from AI.

Enforce free-tier constraints:

Max 3 workspaces

1 material per workspace

Keep system scalable and serverless-friendly.

Technical Goals

Use vector embeddings for semantic grounding.

Prevent hallucinations via retrieval-based prompting.

Validate all AI JSON outputs with schema validation.

Keep architecture modular for future quiz/flashcard expansion.

Maintain low operational cost.

##  Non-Goals (Very Important)

This prevents scope creep.

❌ Not a general-purpose chatbot.

❌ Not a movie database.

❌ Not a collaborative real-time whiteboard (for MVP).

❌ Not a note-taking app like Notion.

❌ Not a file storage service.

❌ Not replacing LMS platforms.

❌ Do not use Redis


Focus is:

AI-driven structured understanding of uploaded material.
